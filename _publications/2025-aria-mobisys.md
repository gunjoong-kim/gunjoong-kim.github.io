**Authors:** Chanyoung Jung\*, Jeho Lee\*, Gunjoong Kim, Jiwon Kim, Seonghoon Park, Hojung Cha (\*Co-first authors)

**Venue:** ACM Annual International Conference on Mobile Systems, Applications, and Services (MobiSys 2025)

**Best Paper Award**

ARIA is a system for real-time Vision Foundation Model (VFM) inference on mobile devices by combining GPU full-frame predictions with NPU dynamic-region updates, achieving 99.9% deadline success and up to 50% accuracy improvement. We designed and implemented comprehensive baselines and conducted evaluations on a self-collected dataset tailored for real-world AR scenarios.
