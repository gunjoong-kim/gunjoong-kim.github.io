abstract: "Vega is the first system to achieve 30 FPS full-scene volumetric video streaming on mobile devices with 3D Gaussian Splatting, leveraging object-level selective computation to reduce data size by 99% while maintaining high visual quality. We developed a mobile-friendly 3DGS video encoding scheme on the server and a mobile-optimized Gaussian Splatting renderer on the client, with a task scheduler distributing workloads across CPU/GPU/NPU to ensure smooth real-time playback on smartphones."
