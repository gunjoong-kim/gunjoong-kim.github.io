**Authors:** Jeho Lee, Gunjoong Kim, Chanyoung Jung, Jaehee Kim, Seonghoo Park, Hojung Cha

**Venue:** European Conference on Computer Systems (EuroSys 2026)

viNPU is a framework to run Vision Transformers efficiently on mobile NPUs by combining sensitivity-guided mixed precision with graph rewrites to keep data on-chip. Across devices, it achieved average 11.5x faster inference, up to 44.9x lower energy, with near-no accuracy loss compared to FP16.
