I am a M.S. student in Computer Science and Engineering at [Yonsei University](https://www.yonsei.ac.kr/), working at the [Mobile Embedded Systems Lab](http://meslab.yonsei.ac.kr/) under the supervision of Prof. Hojung Cha.

My research focuses on **Efficient On-device AI** and **Immersive Computing**. I explore both model-level and system-level approaches to bridge the gap between heavy AI workloads and the limited resources of mobile devices, co-optimizing algorithms and runtime systems to enable responsive and reliable AI experiences in real-world settings. I also investigate system designs that support next-generation immersive applications including augmented reality and volumetric media.
